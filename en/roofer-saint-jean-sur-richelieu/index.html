Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a roof cost in Saint-Jean-sur-Richelieu?+

A roof in Saint-Jean-sur-Richelieu ranges from $8,500 to $24,000. The city offers a wide variety of properties, from century-old homes in Vieux-Saint-Jean to Iberville bungalows and new constructions in the northern sector. Free, no-obligation estimate.

Do you serve both Iberville and Saint-Jean?+

Yes, we cover the entire merged city of Saint-Jean-sur-Richelieu: former Saint-Jean, Iberville, Saint-Luc, L'Acadie, and Saint-Athanase. Just 20 minutes from Marieville, it's one of our primary service areas.

Does my insurance cover roof damage in Saint-Jean?+

Damage caused by sudden events (storms, hail, high winds) is generally covered by your home insurance. Normal wear and tear is not. We can help with the claims process by documenting the damage and providing a detailed estimate to your insurer.

Experienced Roofer in Saint-Jean-sur-Richelieu

Saint-Jean-sur-Richelieu, the second largest city in Montérégie, offers varied housing stock from heritage homes in Vieux-Saint-Jean to modern developments in the northern sector. The city, crossed by the Richelieu River, is known for its International Balloon Festival but also for its harsh winters that put roofs to the test. The Iberville and Saint-Luc neighbourhoods have many 1970s-1990s homes whose roofs are reaching end of life. Just 20 minutes from our Marieville headquarters, we've completed over a hundred projects across greater Saint-Jean.
