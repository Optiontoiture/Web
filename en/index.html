FAQ

Your Questions, Our Answers

How much does a roof replacement cost in Quebec in 2026?+

The cost varies depending on surface area, slope, shingle type and deck condition. For a typical home, expect between $8,000 and $20,000. Contact us for a free and accurate estimate.

What is the difference between 3-tab and architectural shingles?+

3-tab shingles are flat and economical (15-20 years). Architectural shingles are dimensional, more wind-resistant (209 km/h) and last 25-40 years. We recommend architectural for 90% of projects.

What is the best time to replace your roof?+

The ideal season is May to October. However, we can perform emergency repairs year-round. Book early in spring for better date availability.

What warranty do you offer?+

As a GAF Master Elite certified roofer, we offer the Golden Pledge 50-year warranty at 100% replacement value on materials and workmanship — the most comprehensive in the industry.

Do you offer financing?+

Yes! Through our partnership with Flexiti, you can finance your project with monthly payments. Quick approval directly with your estimate.

How long does a replacement take?+

Most residential roofs are completed in 1 to 3 days. We provide a precise timeline in our estimate.

Do I need a permit?+

In most municipalities, a permit is not required for a shingle replacement. We verify your city's requirements.

Do you have an RBQ license?+

Yes, our RBQ license is 5665-8412-01. Active and verifiable at all times. We are also APCHQ members.
